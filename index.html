Professor of Statistics and Data Science

Li Ma

I develop and investigate statistical and machine learning methods for analyzing complex data. My current research spans generative models, nonparametric inference, Bayesian inference and computation. A recent focus of my work is statistical assessment of generative AI models and algorithms.

I am affiliated with the Department of Statistics and the Data Science Institute at the University of Chicago.
